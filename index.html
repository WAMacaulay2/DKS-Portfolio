The Games of Dragon King Studios

Growing up playing video games, I got into programming when I learned that one person could make a game if they just knew how. While I ended up deciding that making games for a living wasn't a practical career choice for me, I did complete and release two games on Google Play under my personal brand, "Dragon King Studios."

These games may not have earned me any money, but they got me my start in computer science and software development, and they can help me out one more time by showcasing my capabilities in web coding.
