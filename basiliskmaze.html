The Basilisk's Maze

View in a larger browser to play the game!

How to play:
Choose a character and set the difficulty of the maze.
There are three settings to change the difficulty.
The harder the maze, the more treasure you can score

Navigate to the center of the maze to find the key,
then look for the exit on the maze's edge.

Beware the Basilisk's Gaze and haunted treasure.
they'll both slow you down.

And above all,
don't get caught by the Basilisk
or he'll eat you up!
